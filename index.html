Process and Services

I dedicate myself to presenting my clients with completed projects that not only meet, but exceed, their initial vision. Knowing what to expect when we work together is critical - here is how I deliver success:

  1. Consultation - We'll start with a conversation. Please share your goals and ideas with me and we'll pinpoint how to best capture your vision.

  2. Proposal and Contract - A detailed project proposal of a plan of action, project cost, and project timeline will be decided. Once you look over and sign the contract, the project will begin.

  3. Creation - As my client you will receive regular updates regarding your project status while I create and test it.

  4. Launch & Support - The final project will be delivered and you can have peace of mind knowing I'll be available for ongoing support.
